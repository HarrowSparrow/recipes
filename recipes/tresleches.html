Tres Leches Cake

Tres leches cake has a soft and ultra moist consistency. It is soaked with a 3 milk mixture and topped with lightly sweetened whipped cream and a strawberry for the daring.

A signifigant dessert in Hispanic culture, 'tres leches' translates literally to 'three milk'.

  • Ingredients

    • 1 Cup all-purpose flour
    • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 5 large eggs
    • 1 cup sugar, divided into 3/4 and 1/4 cups
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1/3 cup whole milk

  • Syrup:

    • 12 oz evaporated milk
    • 9 oz sweetened condensed milk
    • 1/3 cup heavy whipping cream

  • Frosting:

    • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
    • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
    • 1 cup berries to garnish

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF and butter a 9x13 casserole pan. Set three mixing bowls.

  2. In a large bowl, sift together 1 cup flour, 1 1/2 tsp baking powder, and 1/4 salt. Seperate egg whites and yolks into the other two bowls.

  3. Beat wgg yolks with 3/4 cup sugar with an electric mixer on hifh speed, until yolks are a pale yellow (2 minutes). Stir in 1/3 cup milk and 1 tsp vanilla.

  4. Beat egg whites on high speed until soft peaks form (1 minute). With the mixer on, pour in remaining 1/4 cup sugar ad beat on high speed until egg whites are stiff but not dry (1 minute).

  5. Pour egg yolk mixture over the flour mixture and combing gently with a spatula.

  6. Gently fold in egg white mixture with the spatua until just combined. Pour batter into prepared pan and spread to ecen out the surface and bake at 350oF for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

The syrup:

  1. In a large measurinf cup(or a bowl with a pouring lip), combine 1/3 cup heavy whipping cream, 12 oz evaporated milk and 9oz condensed milk. When the cake is cool, pierce the surface all ocer with a fork. Slowly drizzle the milk mixture over the cake.

The frosting

  1. Pour 2 cups cold heacy whipping cream and 2 tbsp sugar into a large chilled mixing bowl and beat on high speed 1 1/2 to 2 minutes or until thick, whipped and spreadable. Spread over the cake with a spatula then decorate with berries if desired.
